ServeBoard Terms of Service

Effective Date: November 5, 2025

ServeBoard is a volunteer coordination tool provided by York Alliance Church (“we,” “us,” or “our”) to help organize and communicate ministry opportunities within our church family and partner organizations.

1. Purpose

ServeBoard exists to make it easy for volunteers to sign up for ministry projects, service opportunities, and community outreach events. Use of this tool is intended for York Alliance Church members, volunteers, and trusted partners.

2. Acceptable Use

By using ServeBoard, you agree to:

  • Provide accurate information when signing up for volunteer opportunities.
  • Treat all contact information and project details shared within ServeBoard as confidential and for ministry purposes only.
  • Use ServeBoard in a way that reflects the values of York Alliance Church and the love of Christ in service to others.

We ask that users do not:

  • Post or share offensive or inappropriate content.
  • Attempt to disrupt or misuse the system.
  • Use information gained from ServeBoard for personal, commercial, or unrelated activities.

3. Accounts and Authentication

ServeBoard uses Microsoft accounts for secure sign-in. Your access may be limited to ministry leaders or volunteers as approved by the church. You are responsible for keeping your account credentials secure and for signing out after use on shared devices.

4. Data and Privacy

All information provided through ServeBoard is handled according to our Privacy Statement. ServeBoard stores only basic contact details and volunteer sign-up data needed to coordinate ministry activities.

5. Availability and Changes

We strive to keep ServeBoard available and reliable, but we may occasionally update, modify, or suspend the service for maintenance or improvement. York Alliance Church reserves the right to make updates or improvements at any time.
