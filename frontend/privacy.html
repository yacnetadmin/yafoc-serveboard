ServeBoard Privacy Statement

Effective Date: November 5, 2025

York Alliance Church (“we,” “us,” or “our”) operates the ServeBoard platform to help coordinate volunteer opportunities across our ministries and partner organizations. This Privacy Statement explains how we handle personal information collected through ServeBoard.

1. Information We Collect

  • Name and email address (used for sign-in and volunteer coordination)
  • Volunteer sign-up selections (e.g., events, time slots, or service opportunities)
  • Basic technical information automatically provided by your browser (such as IP address and device type)

We do not collect sensitive personal information or financial data.

2. How We Use Information

  • Authenticate users through Microsoft accounts
  • Manage volunteer sign-ups and assignments
  • Communicate about ministry opportunities and scheduling

We do not sell, rent, or share your personal information with outside parties for marketing purposes.

3. Data Storage and Security

ServeBoard is hosted using Microsoft Azure and related cloud services.
We take reasonable administrative and technical measures to protect your information from unauthorized access, use, or disclosure.

4. Third-Party Services

Authentication and data storage are handled by Microsoft services (Microsoft Entra ID and Azure). Your use of those services is also governed by Microsoft’s privacy policies.

5. Your Choices

You may request removal of your volunteer information or user account by contacting the ServeBoard administrator at:
info@yorkalliance.org
